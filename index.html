Industries We Serve

Engineering solutions tailored for your specific industry needs

Aerospace Industry

We have complete dedicated solutions for the aerospace industry. ZCC-CT have a toolkit dedicated to this field. We can send you the brochure of these products and in 24-48 hours we can deliver the tools for testing.

Automotive

The cost per part is very important in this area. That's why our solutions, both standard and special combined tools, can bring your costs to the desired level. Solutions with CBN, PCD and carbide plates can be implemented in a relatively short time.

Oil and Gas

The industry requires robust solutions and rigid tools. Our suppliers have a wide range of products that would meet your needs. Thus carbide anti-vibration bars, silent tool-family bars can be a necessary solution.

Die and Molds

A dynamic industry that requires high-performance solutions. Thus carbide tools (solid round tools) are the strength of this industry. Optimizing the process, using the right tool are challenges to which we are always with our partners.

General Engineering

The widest field of mechanical processing. Flexible solutions, the use of modular tools will significantly reduce storage space in stores and ultimately a low cost.
